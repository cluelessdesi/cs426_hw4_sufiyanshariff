game design/idea (same as hw2): <br />
    you are in space and have to steal parts from a gaming PC. After you steal the parts, you have to correctly answer the question about computer architecture. You can hit aliens floating around for hints. <br />

physics constructs: <br />
    falling asteroids -> can hit them causing them to move <br />
    moon -> can shoot at it; turns red when hit <br />
    floating asteroids -> can shoot at them; they don't fall but float further away into space <br />

billboard: <br />
    texture added to billboard is a starry night sky background <br />
    billboard is otherwise same as assignment 2 <br />

lights: <br />
    2 stars, both coloured and fixed in position <br />
    1 is a red star resembling our sun <br />
    1 is a blue dwarf star <br />

how everything fits into my design: <br />
    the game is set in space so I thought I'd add more space elements hence the asteroids, moon and stars <br />
    the falling asteroids pose a risk to the player has to be more careful <br />
    the moon turning red is supposed to show the player they are being hostile to the moon <br />
    the 2 stars being different colours adds a bit more fantastical element to the game <br />
