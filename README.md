game design/idea (same as hw2):
    you are in space and have to steal parts from a gaming PC. After you steal the parts, you have to correctly answer the question about computer architecture. You can hit aliens floating around for hints.

physics constructs:
    falling asteroids -> can hit them causing them to move
    moon -> can shoot at it; turns red when hit
    floating asteroids -> can shoot at them; they don't fall but float further away into space

billboard:
    texture added to billboard is a starry night sky background
    billboard is otherwise same as assignment 2

lights:
    2 stars, both coloured and fixed in position
    1 is a red star resembling our sun
    1 is a blue dwarf star

how everything fits into my design:
    the game is set in space so I thought I'd add more space elements hence the asteroids, moon and stars
    the falling asteroids pose a risk to the player has to be more careful
    the moon turning red is supposed to show the player they are being hostile to the moon
    the 2 stars being different colours adds a bit more fantastical element to the game
